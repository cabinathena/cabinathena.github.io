Rules

Things you should pay attention to
before entering Library of Athena
    Do's
  • Bring your own membership card
  • Toggle on the silent mode of your own D-Watch
  • Keep the library clean
  • Please put the book you read onto its rightful place
  • Please return the book you borrow within its alloted time
  • One person can only borrow up to 3 books at a given time
  • Only 2 person per cabin can borrow books at given time, but there's no quota for reading in-house
    Don'ts
  • Don't make loud noise while inside
  • Soiling or tearing the book is strictly prohibited
  • Don't bring foods and drink inside
  • Don't bring pet or familiar inside, please leash them outside
